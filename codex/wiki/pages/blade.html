Blade

Nimble melee attacks with a blade weapon that can cause bleeding.

Primary Attribute

DEXTERITY

Bleed Roll

After dealing damage with a blade, the target rolls a Dexterity saving throw of 10+ your skill modifier. On a faliure, the enemy now takes 1d4 bleed damage for 3 turns. This effect may stack up to two times, meaning three 3d4 bleed damage.
