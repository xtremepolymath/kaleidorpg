Using spare parts, you can repair weapons by 25% and some broken items.

Action: Drone Scout (Non-Combat/Major)
You send out a small drone up to 300 feet away to give you simple visuals as it moves along. If it is noticed, it will be attacked and all enemies will have +2 to passive perception from then on. When attacked, it has an AC of 8 and 1 HP.
