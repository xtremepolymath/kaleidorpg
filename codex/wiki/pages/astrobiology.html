Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Identify Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Identify action.

Action: Identify (Non-Combat)
Roll to try and glean information about nearby creatures or plant species. 0-10 = No pertinent info, 10-16 = Some info, 16+ = Very useful knowledge. This is used to find specific herbs or perhaps find a weakness in a non-sentient enemy.

5 Healing Herbs You can now perform the Healing Herbs action.

Action: Healing Herbs (Major)
May use healing herbs in inventory to heal a party member for 1d6 plus skill modifier HP.

10 Field Guide Base skill proficiency is now +1.
15 Calm Lifeform You can now perform the Calm Lifeform action.

Action: Calm Lifeform (Major)
Roll against nearby target's INT. On a success, they will cease to be aggressive towards party. Works on hostile creatures or plants. If they are attacked or reaggravated, they will become hostile again.

20 Herbal Tea Healing herbs now heals for 2d6 plus skill mod.

30 Snaring Vines You can now perform the Snaring Vines action.

Action: Snaring Vines (Major)
Anywhere adjacent to you, you lay down snaring vine starters, which take root instantly and lie dormant. The next character (hostile or friendly) to step on that location will be snared by vines and will lose the rest of their turn. Each turn, they are unable to move until they succeed a STR saving throw of 10 plus your skill mod.
