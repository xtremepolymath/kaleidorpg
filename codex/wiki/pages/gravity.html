Gravity

Changing gravitational forces on yourself or others

Primary Attribute

WISDOM

Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Move Object Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Move Object action.

Action: Move Object (Noncombat/Major)
You can levitate and move an object weighing under 20kg to a location of your choice. If used as an attack, you use a charge and can deal 1d8 plus mod damage.

5 Levitate Self You can now perform the Levitate Self action.

Action: Levitate Self (Major)
You levitate yourself up to 1m off the ground. Your movement is limited to 0.5m per turn. Ends after 5 minutes or if you use another Gravity skill.

Slow Momentum You can now perform the Slow Momentum action.

Slow Momentum (Major)
Able to slow down the movement of an object/creature weighing under 150kg by 5m per turn. If a creature is brought to a full stop, it must make a STR saving throw of 10+ your mod to try and break your control. Requires use of one hand to maintain.

15 Telekinesis Move Object now works on objects up to 50 kg, and it deals 2d8 damage as an attack. Also, Slow Momentum works on objects up to 250kg.
20 Deflect You can now perform the Deflect action.

Deflect (Major)
You empower one of your hands to deflect laser fire, giving you a +4 to AC against guns for 5 turns or until you use another Gravity skill. You must have one hand free to do this.
