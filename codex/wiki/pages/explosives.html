Explosives

Make things boom.

Primary Attribute

CONSTITUTION

Proximity

Your explosive ammo has two ranges, one is the full-damage range and the other is half-damage or AOE range. Anyone within the AOE range has a moment to react and try to mitigate the damage. They can perform a DEX saving throw of 12+ your skill mod to quickly dodge to an open adjacent space and try to take less damage.
