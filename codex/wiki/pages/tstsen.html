Ts'tsen

("Ts" pronounced like in "tsunami")

Size
Males: 1.70 - 1.95m
Females: 1.55 - 1.80m
Appearance
The Ts'tsen are of medium height and average frame. They have greyish, scaly skin and do not grow hair. They value being dressed professionally and avoid flamboyance.
Homeworld
Ix'aal
Behavior
Ts'tsen are quick-witted and enjoy high-stakes business and other endeavors. Everything is a competition to them, and sometimes you have to break the rules to win.
Galactic Role
Ts'tsen are the primary business owners and merchants of the Kaleido galaxy, and they've been quick to create alliances with MW species.
Attributes
+2 DEX, +1 CHA, -1 CON
Skills
+10 Espionage, +5 Long Range, +5 Blunt, and +5 to one of your choice
Unique Trait
You can bribe to gain an automatic success on a negotiation check.
