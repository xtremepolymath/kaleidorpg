Muhmin

("Moo-min")

Size
Males: 1.95 - 2.25m
Females: 1.90 - 2.20m
Appearance
Muhmin are large, hairy, and cat-like. They are often equipped with dark goggles to protect their eyes from brighter environments than their homeworld.
Homeworld
Yizke
Behavior
Muhmin have a long history of having to fight for survival. As such, they are aggressive and extremely skilled warriors.
Galactic Role
Muhmin are typically seen as warriors and mercenaries. This is a result of their decades-long war with the Quexi.
Attributes
+2 STR, +1 DEX, -1 CHA
Skills
+10 Athletics, +5 Assault Weapons, +5 Fluids, and +5 to one of your choice
Unique Trait
Able to see clearly in dim lighting and resistant to cold.

If completely unarmed, may choose to run on all fours to gain an extra 5m of movement. Can only perform unarmed attacks while doing this.
