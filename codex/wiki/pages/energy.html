Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Ignite Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Ignite action.

Action: Ignite (Noncombat/Major)
You may pick a 1m sphere of space you can see within 10m to ignite. Fire may spread to other nearby objects. Can be used in combat to burn an enemy for 3 turns of 1d4 fire damage, but cannot stack. Costs 1 stim charge.

5 Bolt You can now perform the Bolt action.

Action: Bolt (Major)
Fire a bolt of electricity that deals 2d6 damage. Costs 1 stim charge.

10 Hot Blooded You take half damage from all heat sources.
15 Energize You can now perform the Energize action.

Action: Energize (Non-Combat/Major)
You focus on absorbing energy from a heat or UV source to become energized. This requires a full uninterrupted turn in combat. The next Energy-based action you take is enerigized, meaning damage or effectiveness is doubled. You may also use the stored energy to recharge on of your own stim charges.

20 Discharge You can now perform the Discharge action.

Action: Discharge (Major)
If you are energized, you may send a wave of energy in a 4m sphere around you dealing 2d6 damage to anything in range, including friendly creatures.
