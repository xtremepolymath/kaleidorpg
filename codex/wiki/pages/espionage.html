Espionage

Being deceptive either through disguise or through sneaking.

Primary Attribute

CHARISMA

Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Sneak Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Sneak action.

Action: Sneak (Non-Combat)
Roll against the highest passive perception of a group of lifeforms to attempt to not be noticed. Doesn't work if you've already been noticed. Lasts until you step into view or make a significant noise. You can also overhear hushed conversations up to 5m away. May grant bonus first attack.

5 Decoy You can now perform the Decoy action.

Action: Decoy (Non-Combat)
You create a holographic image of yourself and program it to perform a specific action on repeat (e.g. walk, sit, say a single phrase, etc.). Will distract nearby intelligent lifeforms until something dirupts the believablility (e.g. being attacked, running into a wall, saying the same phrase over and over). Does not work if the lifeform sees you create the decoy.

10 Rubber Soles Base skill mod is now +1.
15 Sensitive Hearing You can now overhear hushed conversations up to 10m away.
20 Sneak Attack You can now perform the Sneak Attack action.

Action: Sneak Attack (Non-Combat/Major)
While sneaking, you may perform a single sneak attack to a single target with either a gun or melee weapon. You gain advantage to hit and deal double damage. You immediately become noticed by lifeforms you didn't attack.
