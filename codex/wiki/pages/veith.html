Veith

("Vay-th")

Size
Males: 1.65 - 1.90m
Females: 1.50 - 1.75m
Appearance
Veith are short and sleek, and have smooth, bluish-green skin. They grow black hair on their heads, but it is usually pulled back or cut short for swimming.
Homeworld
Veithnu
Behavior
The Veith are a strange and mystical race who are especially adept at stim usage. Though they cannot breath underwater, they spend much of their lives in the plentiful lakes of their homeworld.
Galactic Role
Veith have been travelling their galaxy for centuries and are considered great pilots. They typically have good relationships with other races, even if they can be aloof.
Attributes
+2 WIS, +1 CON, -1 INT
Skills
+10 Energy, +5 Wave, +5 Vehicles, and +5 to one of your choice
Unique Trait
Normal movement underwater. Energy stims have one extra charge.
