Fluids

Manipulation of matter states, especially liquids.

Primary Attribute

WISDOM

Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Hydrate Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Hydrate action.

Action: Hydrate (Noncombat/Major)
You can create a small jet of water originating from one of your hands out up to 10m. It does not cause damage, but can be used to douse fires, water plants, or refill water containers. Does not use a charge.

5 Displace Liquid You can now perform the Displace Liquid action.

Action: Displace Liquid (Major)
You can manipulate a 1m radius sphere of nearby liquid within 10m of your current position. This could be used to create an air-bubble or to bombard a target with a fluid. If used as an attack, you deal 1d8 + skill mod damage.

10 Freezing Touch You can now perform the Freezing Touch action.

Action: Freezing Touch (Major)
You touch an object to freeze it solid. For objects larger than 50kg, you must use a charge. In combat, target must make a CON saving throw of 12+ your modifier. On a fail, they are frozen. Each turn, they must make a CON saving throw to become unfrozen, but the saving throw threshold goes down by 2 each turn.

15 Fog You can now perform the Fog action.

Action: Fog (Non-Combat/Major)
You cause atmospheric liquid to condense and create a fog that covers a cube 20m wide and long and 5m tall.

20 Jet Hydrate's range is increased to 20m. May use a charge to have Hydrate deal 2d6 + mod damage.
