Gleaning information from your situation and surroundings.

Primary Attribute

INTELLIGENCE

Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Investigate Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Investigate action.

Action: Investigate (Non-Combat)
Roll plus skill mod to investigate your surroundings. Depending on the result, the GM will give you information regarding the subject of your investigation. A failed roll, though, might put you at a disadvantage.

5 Keen-Eyed You collect more money, ammo, etc. off of fallen enemies.
10 Perfect Vision Base skill mod is now +1.
15 Thinking Ahead You can now perform the Thinking Ahead action.

Action: Thinking Ahead (Non-Combat/Major)
Roll plus skill mod to think about future possibilities based on a specific or hypothetical lifeform. Depending on the result, the GM will give you insight into what could happen in the future in the format of "If I were…then I would…". Example: "If I were the guard of this building, I wouldn't be too suspicious of the elevator running."

20 Weak Spot You can now perform the Weak Spot action.

Action: Weak Spot (Non-Combat/Major)
Roll plus skill mod to gain insight into a target's possible weakness. On a 0-10, you gain no knowledge. On a 10-15, you choose which piece of information to gain: current HP, elemental weakness, or weak spot. On a 16+, you also gain advantage on next three attacks against that and similar targets. Can only be done once per type of target per day.
