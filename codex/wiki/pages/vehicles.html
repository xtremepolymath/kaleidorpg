1 Swerve Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Swerve action.

Action: Swerve (Non-Combat/Major)
On a failed vehicles check, you may re-roll the check to try to regain control, but with -2 to the final result.

5 Grease Monkey You can now perform the Grease Monkey action.

Action: Grease Monkey (Non-Combat)
You can repair vehicles that have more than 50% HP without having to use spare parts for 2d8 HP.

10 Motorhead Base skill mod now 1. No longer at disadvantage handling alien vehicles
15 Ramming Speed You can now perform the Ramming Speed action.

Action: Ramming Speed (Major)
If you have more than half vehicle movment remaining this turn, you may use the rest of your vehicle movement and a major action to ram and deal damage to enemies or obstacles. Enemies not in vehicles roll DEX saving throw of 12+ skill mod, on a failure, you deal 4d6 damage. On a success, you deal no damage, but they must move 2 spaces away. For obstacles, including other vehicles, you roll a saving throw of 18- skill mod. On a success, roll 3d6. You deal that much damage to your vehicle and twice that amount to the obstacle. On a failure, damage is equal.

20 Evasive Maneuver You can now perform the Evasive Maneuver action.

Action: Evasive Maneuver (Non-Combat/Major)
All enemies must roll a dex saving throw of 12+ skill mod. On a failure, they are at a disadvantage for every hit roll against you for three turns.
