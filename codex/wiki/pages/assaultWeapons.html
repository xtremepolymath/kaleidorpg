Assault Weapons

High damage output possibility, but is limited by range and accuracy.

Primary Attribute

STRENGTH

Spray Roll

Every successful ranged attack with an assault weapon, roll a d6 before you roll damage dice. On a 0 you deal no damage, on a 1 or 2 you deal half damage, on a 3 or 4 you deal normal damage, on a 5 or 6 you deal 1.5 times damage (rounded down). Each subsequent spray roll this turn, you must subtract an additional -1 from the die result. For instance, if you have three attacks this turn, your first Spray roll will have no change, the second spray roll will be a -1 to the result, and the third spray roll will be a -2 to the result. Some perks or mods may alter the spray roll.
