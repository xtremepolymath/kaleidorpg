Unarmed

Dealing damage and controlling the battle with just your fists.

Primary Attribute

STRENGTH

Gloves

The unarmed skill utilizes your equipped gloves to deal blows. You must have at least one of your hands unequipped to perform an unarmed attack. With Power Gauntlets, you cannot equip any other weapon at the same time.

Sweeping Kick (Major)
Roll to hit and deal 1d6 damage plus skill mod. Enemy makes a DEX saving throw of 12+ your skill mod. On a failure, they are knocked prone giving you advantage against them until they stand up and forcing an attack of opportunity when they stand.
