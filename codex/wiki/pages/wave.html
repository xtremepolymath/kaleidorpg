Perk List

Level Name Description
1 Illuminate Base skill mod is now 0. You can now perform the Illuminate action.

Action: Illuminate (Noncombat)
An orb of soft light appears in your hands and illuminates in a 10m sphere. You may either keep the light indefinitely with free hand, or you may cast the light in a single direction. When cast, it will dissipate when it hits an object. Does not use a charge.

5 Throw Voice You can now perform the Throw Voice action.

Action: Throw Voice (Major)
You can make your voice come from a location up to 25m away for up to 5 minutes.

10 Night Vision You now have full vision in dim light and limited vision at night. Muhmin have full vision in any lighting except absolute darkness.
15 Refract You can now perform the Refract action.

Action: Refract (Major)
You manipulate the light around you to refract, causing you to be harder to see for 1 minute. You are invisible when standing still, and Observation checks are at disadvantage when you're moving.

20 Harmonic Frequency You can now perform the Harmonic Frequency action.

Action: Harmonic Frequency (Major)
You place a hand on the ground and radiate the ground's harmonic frequency causing the ground to shake. Everything in a 10m radius must make a CON saving throw of 10+. On a failure, they are knocked prone.
