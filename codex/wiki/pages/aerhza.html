Aerhza

("Ayr-zuh")

Size
Males: 1.60 - 1.82m
Females: 1.50 - 1.75m
Appearance
Aerhza are slightly smaller than humans. They have a light violet skin and typically grow their dark hair into long braids.
Homeworld
Enneon
Behavior
Aerhza are a peaceful and curious species. They enjoy congregating between themselves and other species, and try to quell disagreements as quickly as possible.
Galactic Role
They are acclaimed diplomats and scientists, though they can also be formidable warriors when called upon to do so.
Attributes
+2 CHA, +1 INT, -1 DEX
Skills
+10 Negotiation, +5 Astrobiology, +5 Psyche, and +5 to one of your choice
Unique Trait
Once per NPC, they may reroll a negotiation check.
