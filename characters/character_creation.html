CYBORG
Size
Males: 1.70 - 1.95m
Females: 1.55 - 1.8m
Appearance
Bipedal and slender-framed. They resemble humans, but have metallic augmentations ranging from single body parts to their entire body.
Homeworld
Earth
Behavior
Cyborgs will either behave exactly like humans or in a more robotic and calculated manner depending on the nature of their augmentations.
Galactic Role
Cyborgs are more specialized than humans, though that speciality anything depending on their augmentations. Some cyborgs see themselves as a better version of humans, but they can experience animosity from other races who consider them "unatural".
Attributes
+2 to one of your choice
Skills
+10 to two that relate to your augmentations
+5 to Tech
Unique Trait
You may heal up to 25% of your own HP using the Patch Up action.
QUEXI
Size
Males: 1.80 - 2.14m
Females: 1.72 - 1.85m
Appearance
Bipedal, tall, and very slender. They are hairless and have a waxy skin that ranges from nearly white to yellow and orange.
Homeworld
Quexond
Behavior
Quexi are extremely intelligent but also very individualistic. Their tendencies and early spaceflight have led them to prefer space-bound over planetary habitation.
Galactic Role
Quexi are very scientifically and technologically focused, making them excellent pilots and and gatherers of information. To compensate for evolving in a low-gravity environment, they are adept with Grav stimplants.
Attributes
+1 Int, +1 Dex
Skills
+10 Tech, +5 Vehicles, +5 Pistol, and +5 Grav
Unique Trait
Not at disadvantage for skill checks in zero gravity.
