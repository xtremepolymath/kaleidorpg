BACKGROUND

Your character's background is a simple description of what they are or do up to the start of the campaign. This could be a career, an occupation, a title, or something else. This may have little or no impact on gameplay but will help give your character some color.

Feel free to use one of the backgrounds from the provided list or come up with one of your own.

Colonist Criminal Doctor Figurehead Gladiator Mechanic Mercenary Merchant Researcher Savant Soldier Spiritualist Techie

ALIGNMENT

Your allignment is how your character treats morals and ethics. A lawful character will try to stick to a set of rules, be they good or evil. A chaotic character will prefer to bend or break the rules, either for the common good or for their own benefit.

Choose the alignment that best suits your character:

Lawful Good Lawful Neutral Lawful Evil
Neutral Good True Neutral Neutral Evil
Chaotic Good Chaotic Neutral Chaotic Evil
ATTRIBUTES

Attributes are used for skill modifiers and some saving throws. Each attribute will have a modifier based on its level. This modifier may be added to skill depending on the skill and an unlocked expertise.

All attributes start out as 10 and have a modifier of 0. Every two levels, you will be able to increase one of your attributes by 1. For creating a character, the race you chose will affect your attributes, and then you can subtract or add from each attribute as you like. You cannot have an attribute go lower than 2 or higher than 30.
